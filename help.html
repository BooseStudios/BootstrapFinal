How to Help

1.
Play Fallout 4

Fallout 4 is set in an alternate universe where everyone gets nuked and people have to adapt to the post apocalyptic wasteland. This is great to study from in the event of total destruction of our planet, how to prepare for the aftermath.

2.
Recycle

Although it may not seem like it, recycling does help the environment. Imagine a cleaner life, done imagining? Start making it happen.

3.
Burn Down Factories

Factories are a huge contributor in climate change. More and more of them continue to be built, what can we do about it? Well burn them to the ground one by one of course!

4.
Start Walking

Cars produce a large amount of gas, the solution? Start walking. No matter how far you have to travel, it could be hundreds of miles and it would take you weeks to reach your destination but at least you will be saving the environment!

5.
Get Off Your Phone

Phones make people lazy. Laziness is a big reason why very few people are trying to make the world a better place. So get off the phone and take a stroll over to the person you were going to call or text. Face to face interaction is much better.

6.
Don't Panic

Panicking is the universal code for everything is falling apart, I might as well fall apart too. When you panic, everyone else does too, which can alter how earth will form in a negative way. Always stay positive and bring positive vibes to the world.
